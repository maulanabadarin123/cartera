Getting Started with Cartera

To begin your journey with Cartera, follow this step-by-step guide to download the app, set up your account, fund your wallet, and initiate trades.

Step 1: Download the Cartera App

The first step to accessing all that Cartera has to offer is to download the app. You can find the Cartera app in the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Simply search for "Cartera," and click on the download button to install it on your device. Ensure that you have a stable internet connection for a smooth download experience.

Step 2: Set Up Your Account

After installing the application, launch it to initiate the account creation process. To begin using Cartera Wallet, an NFC card is required as the access key. This can be a debit card, gamestation card, or any national ID card equipped with NFC technology.

Step 3: Fund Your Wallet

With your account set up, the next step is to fund your Cartera wallet. Navigate to the wallet section of the app, where you will find various options to add funds. You can choose to deposit cryptocurrency or use a connected bank account for transferring fiat currency. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the funding process. Be aware that different funding methods may have varying processing times and fees.

Step 4: Initiate Trades

Now that your wallet is funded, you are ready to start trading. Go to the trading section of the app, where you can view available assets and market data. Select the asset you wish to trade, specify the amount, and choose whether you want to buy or sell. After reviewing the details, confirm your trade. Cartera provides real-time updates, so you can track your trades and market movements seamlessly.
By following these steps, you will be well on your way to utilizing Cartera effectively for your digital asset management and trading needs.
